Introduction

ChronicCare - Diet & Health Monitor ("we", "us", or "the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our mobile application. We believe in transparency and data ownership, which is why we have designed ChronicCare to be a Privacy-First application.

1. Information We Collect

We collect only the information necessary to provide the core features of the App. This includes:

  • Health Data: Information you manually enter, such as meals, blood pressure, weight, symptoms, and health notes.
  • Camera and Photos: With your permission, the App accesses your camera or photo library to allow you to take or select photos of your food for nutritional analysis.
  • App Settings: Your preferences and configurations within the App.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to provide and improve the services within the App:

  • Personal Health Monitoring: To display your health trends, meal history, and insights locally on your device.
  • AI Food Analysis: When you upload a food photo, the image is processed to identify food items and estimate nutritional content.
  • No Profiling: We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or selling to third parties.

3. Data Storage and Security

Your privacy is guaranteed by the architecture of the App:

  • Local Storage: All health records, food logs, and personal data are stored exclusively on your device using a local SQLite database and AsyncStorage.
  • No Cloud Sync: We do not maintain any cloud servers to store your health data. Your data never leaves your device unless you explicitly export it.
  • Data Safety: Because your data is local, the security of your information depends on the security of your mobile device. We recommend using your device's built-in security features (Passcode, FaceID, TouchID).

4. Third-Party Services

To provide advanced AI features, we utilize the following third-party service:

  • SiliconFlow AI API: When you request AI food analysis, the food image is sent to SiliconFlow's servers for temporary processing. The image is used only for analysis and is not stored or linked to any personal identity by us.

Apart from the AI processing mentioned above, no health data is shared with any third-party providers.

5. Your Rights and Control

You have full control over your data:

  • Access and Export: You can view all your logged data within the App at any time.
  • Deletion: You can delete individual records or use the "Clear All Data" feature in settings to wipe all information from the local database.
  • App Removal: Deinstalling the App will permanently delete all data stored within it, as we do not keep backups on our servers.
  • Permissions: You can revoke camera or photo access at any time through your device's system settings.

6. Children's Privacy

ChronicCare is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with health data, please delete the data from the device or contact us.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy within the App and updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page. Your continued use of the App after changes constitute your acceptance of the new terms.
